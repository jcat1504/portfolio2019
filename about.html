03 : About me

Hello!

My name is Jessica but I prefer to be called Jess. I became a software developer in August 2017 and have loved it ever since. My reason for pursuing software development is quite surprising...I pursued it for the steep learning curve and my drive for learning has helped me understand learning the foundation of programming languages I come to know and love today. I guess you can say it was my curiosity that became part of my self-motivation!

Why Software Development?

One reason is that I get to express my creative side. I was a graphic design major in college. I loved it but I felt as if I wanted to try something new. I wanted to change career paths. I grew up loving art and love to draw, paint, craft cosplay, and of the like. I wanted to learn the technical side of software and web, rather than creating it. So my curiosity stemmed from my previous love for graphic design.

Another reason is that self-taught developers can also land a job. Companies out there don't necessarily require a degree from a top-tier university as long as you possess the skills and mindset. Some other reasons include: flexibility, technology will always stay relevant, and software developers are needed EVERYWHERE.
